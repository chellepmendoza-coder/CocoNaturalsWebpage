Created with Love & Purpose · Rooted in Filipino Tradition

Our Story

Coco Chelle Naturals was born from a journey of healing, heritage, and hope. As a solo parent, I found strength in the coconut oil rituals passed down by my beloved Lilang. Her grace and care inspired every product we create.

Our Mission

We honor Filipino traditions while offering gentle, nourishing care for hair and skin. Every bottle is a tribute to love, resilience, and natural beauty.
