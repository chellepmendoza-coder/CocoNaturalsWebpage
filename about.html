Our Story

Coco Chelle was born from a journey of healing. After facing grief, depression, and hair loss, I returned to the rituals my Lilang lovingly practiced—massaging coconut oil into my scalp, whispering prayers, and reminding me of my strength.

Every bottle we offer is a reflection of that love. It’s more than a product—it’s a legacy. A way to honor Filipino tradition and help others rediscover their own beauty and resilience.
